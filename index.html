About My Campus

Hacktiv8 is a learning program that focuses on the IT field. The program consists of Full Time Program namely Full Stack JavaScript Immersive which is the first bootcamp program in Indonesia. Hacktiv8 also has a variety of short programs to build the foundation of being a reliable programmer in the Part Time Program class. The Hacktiv8 curriculum is designed to build expertise that can be applied to the development of the latest technological world.

Why Big Industries Recruit Coding Bootcamp Graduates?

Coding Bootcamp is there to provide solutions to the high demand for talented labour in technology. With an intensive program, they are able to produce quality graduates even without the titles of a computer degree. To become a professional in their field, one does not have to carry an academic degree in his name. An intensive learning process with the concept of learning by doing is in fact more effective in producing proficient programmers in a short period.


From a companies view, who doesn't want to employ people with extraordinary talents and potential? Recruiting and hiring graduates from Coding Bootcamp will bring its own benefits for employers. Here is the reason.
